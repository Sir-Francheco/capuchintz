Parish Ministry

The primary mission of the first Capuchin missionary in Tanzania was to establish a local church in Tanzania. In this they built many churches and did parish ministry in different parts of Tanzania. After a time the mission was quite successful and the brothers had a great impact to the local church. This resulted for there to be parishes run by the Capuchin Friars even a long time after the mission success.

As for the year 2021 The Capuchin friars in Tanzania were present in 22 parishes in all the geographical zones in Tanzania. The following is the list of the Parishes run by the Capuchin friars minor of the province of Tanzania.
